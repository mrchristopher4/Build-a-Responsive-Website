Explore Your Passion in the NYC Salsa in The Parks Series

Tired of Being Couped Up in the Dance Studio?

There's dancing in the streets, and the parks, of New York City. A tradition since 2014, NYC Salsa in The Parks Series returns to NYC this summer! Join an outdoor dance party or watch a dance performance by performers from around the world in the very parks of NYC.

Our series makes dance fun and easy for all ages and ability levels with free hands-on instruction in popular steps. Audiences will learn basic moves in styles like salsa and swing, bachata, salsa dura, and more. Whether you’re a novice or an advanced dancer, every outdoor dance party starts with beginner Salsa lessons before becoming a sizzling party under the night sky & shimmering NYC lights — all while learning a new move or two!

Expert Instruction from Some of the Finest Salsa Instructors

Talia Castro-Pozo is the producer of Latin Mondays at Taj, Bryant Park Dance Party, Sunset Salsa with Talia to name a few. Her warm, fun & inclusive approach to dance events, enables New Yorkers to fall in love with Latin Dance & Latin Culture. She's also an accomplished dancer, actress & choreographer. Talia is currently playing "Mona" for the TV mini series "Fleishman Is In Trouble" co-starring Jessie Eisenberg & Claire Danes.

Marlon Mills, also joyfully known as Marlon International, is one of New York City’s most well-known dance educators and dance personalities in the Salsa dance community. He has trained with some of the best in the business, including Maria and Eddie Torres, and has been teaching salsa for more than 20 years. Marlon has performed Salsa for films, television shows, festivals, and clients such as Marriott Group and American Express, and on stage for artists such as Jose Alberto El Canario, Hector Tricoche, and El Gran Combo.
